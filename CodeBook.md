This code book describes the resulting data fields in tidyData.txt. 
tidyData.txt is generated by executing run_analysis.R script against data downloaded from https://d396qusza40orc.cloudfront.net/getdata%2Fprojectfiles%2FUCI%20HAR%20Dataset.zip. 
The data measured are the average values of all the measurements on the mean and standard deviation for each measurement and grouped by Subject and Activity.

##Identifiers

Subject - The ID of the subject who performed the activities. There are total 30 subjects identified by numbers 1 to 30.
Activity - Type of activity performed when the corresponding measurements were taken. There are total 6 activities. They are

1) WALKING 
2) WALKING_UPSTAIRS 
3) WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS 
4) SITTING 
5) STANDING 
6) LAYING
